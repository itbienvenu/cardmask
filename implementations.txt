Key Professional Implementations:
Strategic Masking Logic:
Merchant Locking: 
You can now create a card restricted to a specific merchant (e.g., "Netflix"). If used elsewhere, the transaction is instantly declined.
Disposable Cards (ONE_TIME): 
Cards that automatically block themselves after a single successful transaction, perfect for "shady" or unknown websites.
Spending Limits: 
Each mask card has its own "budget" (limit_amount) separate from the user's total balance.
Professional Transaction Engine:
Real-time Authorization: 
The system now runs a 3-step check for every payment: Status (Active/Blocked), Limits (Available funds), and Merchant Permission.
Integrated Log History: 
Every attempt (Success or Decline) is logged in a persistent Audit Trail, allowing businesses to track suspicious activity.
Reason Tracking: 
Transactions now store a failureReason (e.g., MERCHANT_NOT_AUTHORIZED or CARD_BLOCKED) to help the user understand why a payment failed.
Realistic Fintech Identity:
Chas Bank BINS: 
Generated Visa cards now use the realistic Chase Bank prefix (414720) to look authentic to standard payment gateways.
Vault Simulation: 
Secure data handling by storing only the original_card_last4 and a vault_token for the real card.
